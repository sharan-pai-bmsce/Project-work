    PLEASE MAKE A NOTE

  • Before submitting your paper please ensure that it has been carefully read for typographical and grammatical errors.
  • Papers must not be sent in PDF format.Please note that the folder should not be zipped.
  • The page size should be A4 with margins of 2.54cm all around. Please do not insert headers and footers.
  • Put the title of the paper in bold, left aligned, at the top of the first page only.
    Then, on the next line put the names of the authors.
    Where all authors are from the same institute, the following format should be used.
    Lead Author, Co-author One, Co-Author Two Institute, Town, Country leadauthor@email.com,
    ​co-authorone@email.com ,co-authortwo@email.com
  • Do not use more than three levels of heading and use the numbering convention:
    1 Heading 1
    1 Heading 1
      1.1.1 Heading 3 and so on
  • The main body of the text should be in Calibri 12 point. 
  • You can include bulleted or numbered lists.`
    Avoid colour diagrams as the proceedings will be printed in black and white.
    Images must be inserted as picture files (.gif, .jpg, .jpeg). 
    ou may be asked to supply the pictures as separate files.​
  • References should follow the Harvard referencing style,
    1. Brooks, I. and Weatherston, J. (1997) The Business Environment: Challenges and Changes, Prentice Hall, London.
    2. Dolan, S.L., Garcia, S. and Auerbach, A. (2003) “Understanding and Managing Chaos in Organisations”, International
      Journal of Management, Vol 20, No. 1, pp 23–35.
    3. Goldberg, J. and Markoczy, L. (1998) “Complex Rhetoric and Simple Games”, [online], Cranfield University,/link/
