2020 ACADEMIC CONFERENCE

Conference paper can be effective way to try out new ideas,introduce your to the colleagues and hone your research questions. Presenting at a conference is a great way to get valuable feedback from a community of scholars and for increasing you prefessional stature in your field. So get ready to widen your horizen!

Why To Apply?

Conferences are an essential part of academic life, but with all of the focus on getting papers accepted and the stress of presenting or giving talks, they can sometimes feel like an overwhelming or even scary experience. This, however, should not deter you, there are countless great reasons to attend a conference!

  • Get feedback on an early version of your latest work
  • Get to know other people in your field
  • Hear about the latest research
  • Improve your presentation and communication skills
  • Adding to your CV
